F.A.Q

  • What is...NCCBCWCYCC?? What’s the acronym again?? Who is it for? What’s happening????

    The NWCYC (North West Coptic Youth Convention) started in 1993 to bring the Coptic Orthodox youth of Western Canada and the Pacific NorthWest of the USA together in worship, spiritual growth, and fellowship, now under the auspices of His Grace Bishop Mina of the Diocese of Mississauga, Vancouver, and Western Canada. The 2023 NWCYC is for youth and graduates 18 and up (for our insurance’s purposes).

  • Sign me up!!! When can I register? How can I register? What if I need to cancel?

    • The only method to register is through https://linktr.ee/nwcyc or contacting your church servants listed below (there is no registration through the venue). -Registration rates reflect a $100/person diocese subsidy already applied.
    • Early Bird registration deadline: Tuesday June 20 -Regular registration deadline: Thursday July 6 -Cancellation deadline for both types of registration for a full refund is Thursday July 6, 2023 (minus a Paypal processing fee of $10 or less).
    • Cancellations and refunds after July 6 are subject to finding someone to replace you.
    • All refunds will be processed in one batch a week or so after the convention, no earlier refunds are possible.

  • What does the price include?

    • -Your stay in your own private bedroom (sharing a bathroom with someone from the same gender, most likely from your church).
    • Your meals: 1 on Friday, 2 on Saturday, 2 on Sunday, 1 on Monday, plus snacks throughout.

  • What’s transportation like?

    Transportation is not included in the convention cost, and may be an extra cost depending on your church. You will hear from your church servants about ride arrangements and their costs, PLEASE inform your church servants (listed below) if you want to plan your own ride.

  • 5. What’s the schedule?

    Working on it! The finalized schedule will be posted on https://linktr.ee/nwcyc a few days before the convention, make sure to check it throughout the convention. We include 2 liturgies and tasbeha, lectures, sports time, and lots of free time. -The convention starts on Friday July 21 around 6 pm, and ends on Monday July 24 at around 12 pm.

  • I'm ready to pack my bags! What do I bring??

    The venue provides towels, bedding (sheets and duvet cover), and pillows. -Pack lightly (i.e. only one duffle bag) for shared transportation. Include gym clothes and runners, appropriate clothing (with 1 change of clothes halfway through each day), loose clothing to fight the heat. Liturgy items: Boys: pack tonias, girls: pack head scarves, everyone brings lafayef (napkins).
